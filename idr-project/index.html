About IDR

Where Industry Expertise
Meets Academic Rigour

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles across regulated, high-consequence environments. We exist to close the critical skills gap between the demand for digital risk expertise and the supply of qualified, deployment-ready professionals.

Our model combines the depth of UK university research partnerships with the pragmatism of live industry projects. From structured academies and bootcamps to AI governance innovation and advisory services — every practitioner IDR trains is not just educated, but ready to deploy from day one.
